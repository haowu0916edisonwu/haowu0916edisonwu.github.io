I study reliable and verifiable AI generation — when and why should a model commit to its evidence, and how do we verify that it does? My work spans RAG robustness and grounded 3D medical report generation, with a paper under review at ACM MM '26 and a manuscript in submission to ACM TIST. My longer-term interest is extending spatial grounding techniques toward embodied AI and autonomous driving.

我研究可靠且可验证的AI生成——模型何时应对其引用的证据负责，以及我们如何验证？研究涵盖RAG鲁棒性与有依据的3D医学报告生成，目前一篇论文在ACM MM '26审稿中，另一篇正投稿至ACM TIST。长期兴趣方向是将空间定位技术延伸至具身AI与自动驾驶。

02 · GROUNDED GENERATION
02 · 有依据的生成
Verifiable 3D Medical Reporting
可验证3D医学报告生成

Every generated sentence must cite the exact spatial tokens it draws from — evidence commitment by construction. → MARIE, ACM MM '26. This token-gating mechanism is a direct analogue to grounding VLA planning outputs in BEV tokens for autonomous driving and robotic manipulation.

每条生成句子须引用其所依赖的空间token，从设计层面保障证据承诺。→ MARIE, ACM MM '26

04 · EMBODIED AI & AUTONOMOUS SYSTEMS
04 · 具身AI与自动驾驶
Spatial Grounding → VLA & Embodied AI
空间定位 → VLA与具身智能

A future direction I am actively pursuing: applying spatial token grounding techniques to embodied AI and autonomous driving, where VLA models face the same core challenge of anchoring planning outputs to explicit perceptual evidence. The MARIE evidence commitment mechanism provides a direct conceptual bridge.

我正在积极探索的未来方向：将空间token定位技术延伸至具身AI与自动驾驶领域。VLA模型面临与MARIE相同的核心挑战——如何将规划输出锚定于明确的感知证据，MARIE的证据承诺机制为此提供了直接的概念桥梁。
